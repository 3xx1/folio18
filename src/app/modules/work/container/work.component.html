SFMOMA: Magritte Interpretive Gallery

Interactive experiences as part of the SFMOMA Magritte exhibition in 2018: The Fifth Season. Each experience represents his philosophy and perspective with the current state of technology.

Finger Drumming Orchestra

A finger shaped robot that remembers, imitates and repeats your rhythm. As a swarm of multiple fingers, they're wirelessly connected and collectively play orchestrated gorgeous rhythm patterns.

Roygbiv

An experimental music video, programatically generated with a few different algorithmic effects: deep learning style transfer, fluid simulation, and computer vision.

Protophone

One day prototype feature phone for a design research in Malawi, Africa. We developed a simple hardware with a flexible software that is easily modifiable in the field.

Dropnotes

Dropnotes is a music composition table with color water droplets and projection mapping glass surface. Steps to compose music are designed to be easier and more intuitive than a software digital audio workstation.

Arc

Arc is a computer numerical controlled tool for ceramics. As a part of research project, we ran a design research in a pottery studio with the prototype.

Trace

Trace is a mobile application that facilitates guided wandering rather than destination-oriented travel.

Puppetto

Papetto is a lightweight robotic arm that is attachable to anything (such as a phone) and moves according to face detection techniques in order to mirror facial movements.

Shelltone: a figure and a scenery

An artwork inspired by Jean Cocteau's poem: "My ears are shells, fondly hearing the sound of the sea". A computer aided sculpture to sonify a soundscape in a digital landscape.

A/V Pixels

A swarm of small computers that translate sound to colors, which are distributable to a space and provide a capability of visualizing soundscape in it.
